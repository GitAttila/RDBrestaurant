Restaurant Rang De Basanti

Indian restaurant Rang De Basanti is located in Dolní Počernice, Prague.

In Rang De Basanti, we believe that we are a tradition bearer in Indian cuisine, but we are also careful to ensure that the food we offer is always prepared with fresh and high-quality ingredients and that we are not forgetting the health of our guests, so they can enjoy our food without worrying about high cholesterol content. In our Indian restaurant in Dolní Počernice we handle meal orders within radius of up to three kilometers free of charge. We are open to renting our space, including providing a full catering service to celebrate different occasions such as weddings and various other anniversaries.
